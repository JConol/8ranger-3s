Love is not cringe because it is a fundamental human experience that fosters connection, compassion, and understanding. It transcends societal norms or trends and reflects genuine care and vulnerability, which are essential for building meaningful relationships. While expressions of love might sometimes be seen as awkward or over-the-top, the sentiment itself is deeply valuable and universal, making it far from something to dismiss or ridicule. Everyone should love because love is a powerful force that enhances personal well-being and fosters positive relationships. It creates connections that give life meaning, builds trust, and promotes understanding and empathy. Love encourages personal growth by teaching patience, forgiveness, and resilience. On a broader scale, love can bridge divides, unite communities, and inspire acts of kindness that make the world a better place. It enriches the human experience, offering joy, support, and a sense of belonging. Everyone deserves love because it is a fundamental aspect of human existence that nurtures emotional well-being and fosters a sense of belonging. Love provides support, acceptance, and care, which are essential for personal growth and resilience. It validates a person’s inherent worth, reminding them they are valued and appreciated. No matter their background, experiences, or mistakes, love empowers individuals to heal, thrive, and contribute positively to the world. Everyone's right to love reflects the shared humanity that connects us all. LOVE?

Harana is a beautiful expression of Filipino culture that brings a sense of nostalgia and romance. Originating during the Spanish colonial period, it involves suitors serenading their beloveds outside their homes, creating an intimate and enchanting atmosphere. The simple melodies and heartfelt lyrics, often accompanied by a guitar, convey deep emotions of love and longing. Even as times have changed, Harana remains a cherished tradition that highlights the importance of romance and connection in Filipino society, making it a timeless symbol of love.HARANA SONGS: I've Fallen for You: By Jamie Rivera Maging Sino Ka Man: By Arnee
